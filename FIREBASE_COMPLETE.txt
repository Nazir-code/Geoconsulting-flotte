📊 BEFORE VS AFTER
═════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

BEFORE (In-Memory dataStore.js)
  ❌ Data lost on restart
  ❌ Single instance only
  ❌ No backup
  ✅ ~0ms response

AFTER (Firebase Firestore)
  ✅ Data persists forever
  ✅ Multi-instance support
  ✅ Automatic backups
  ✅ ~50-100ms response
  ✅ Auto-scales to millions
  ✅ 99.99% uptime SLA
  ✅ No server management
